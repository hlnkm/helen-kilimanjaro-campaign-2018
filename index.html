Hello friends, family, and internet!

My name is Helen Kim and I'm going to climb Mt. Kilamanjaro's Northern Circuit - the longest route up the mountain - to raise awareness and funding for Parkinson's research.

My funding goal is $19,341 - $1 for each foot of elevation gain. All of your contributions will go directly to funding cutting edge research supported by the Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research.

Let's help find a cure!

THE JOURNEY

MOTIVATION. In 200X my father (late, 2015) was diagnosed with Parkinson's disease. The years following were trying times for me and family, but we are happy knowing he was able to meet his 3 beautiful grandchildren who loved him very much. For those of you who knew Michael Kim, you know that he lived to dance, cook, smile, laugh, and bring joy to those around him. While my father is no longer with us, there's still much to be researched to find treatments and cures for the terrible thing that is Parkinson's.

TRAINING. Since January of 2018, I've been preparing for the climb. Along with long and consistent hours training at the gym, I have been learning about the terrain and preparing gear and materials for the trip to Tanzania.

CLIMB. I will be taking the Northern Circuit Route up Mt. Kilamanjaro. It is not only the longest, but the most beautiful with some of the highest success rates (I don't plan on failure!). My trek will take place between July 25, 2018 to August 1, 2018.

I will be broadcasting my progress through a GPS tracker which you can follow along here.

The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research

Five million people worldwide are living with Parkinson's disease - a chronic, degenerative neurological disorder. Parkinson's really sucks - it's hard to diagnose and basically affects your ability to produce dopamines - yeah, you know those chemicals in your brain that signal motivation and motor control - and in the United States, 60,000 new cases will be diagnosed this year alone.

There is no known cure for Parkinson's disease.

But through your support of Team Fox, you can help change this. Team Fox is the grassroots community fundraising program at The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research (MJFF). (yes Marty McFly from Back to the Future has Parkinson's) MJFF accelerates high-impact science through smart risk taking and problem solving with a commitment to urgency and efficiency.

I'm one of thousands of people around the globe who are turning their passions into Team Fox fundraisers to help them speed a cure for Parkinson's. When you contribute to my campain, 100% of Team Fox proceeds go straight to MJFF research programs
